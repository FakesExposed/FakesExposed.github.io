About

This site is a middle ground for PlayStation scene sites to identify user who create clickbait or fake content meant to mislead the community or generate revenue from said content.

A "profile" is created for accused (with proof) users where site representitives can endorse if they believe that user falls into this category.

Currently under development is a browser extension that will block/redirect a users content thus depriving them of views.
